    Hello, and welcome to my personal website! I'm a native Bostonian and a lover of art, animals, basketball (go C's!), motorcycling, music, whiskey, and gaming. Currently working at MIT, and am always looking to learn new technologies in addition to improving my existing skills.


    Professionally, I'm a data analyst at heart – experienced with both research and visualizations for business, program, and marketing needs. Across my journey, I've had the opportunity to work within both non-profit and for-profit sectors. This diverse experience provided me with the advantages of both worlds, and I'm able to apply the knowledge I've learned to all of my tasks and responsibilities. I'm fortunate enough to have been exposed to academic data, biographical data, financial data, marketing data, etc. and have no issues with communicating and/or presenting actional insights to a variety of individuals – be it C-level executives and stakeholders or any other constituents.

I like to continue learning other languages while also maintaining my current skills so that I don't get too rusty, and figured that R was still perfect for statistics and other similar analyses while I use Python for automation and general cleanup and transformations. Aside from that, I also use Python for a lot of other ad-hoc projects and requests too, including web scraping, automation, and more.
