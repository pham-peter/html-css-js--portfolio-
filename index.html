    Hello, and welcome to my personal website! I'm a native Bostonian and a lover of art, animals, basketball (go C's!), motorcycling, music, whiskey, and gaming. Currently working at MIT, and am always looking to learn new technologies in addition to improving my existing skills.


    Professionally, I'm a data analyst at heart – experienced with both research and visualizations for business, program, and marketing needs. Across my journey, I've had the opportunity to work within both non-profit and for-profit sectors. This diverse experience provided me with the advantages of both worlds, and I'm able to apply the knowledge I've learned from both sectors to all of my tasks and responsibilities. I'm fortunate enough to have been exposed to academic data, biographical data, financial data, marketing data, etc. and have no issues communicating and/or presenting actional insights to a variety of individuals – be it C-level executives and stakeholders or any other constituents.
